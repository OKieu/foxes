About Squirrels

A squirrel is one of the most common rodent that exist in the UK. Their family name is called Sciuiridae. They are indigenous to other continents such as Europe, Africa and North & South America.

Habitat

Squirrels are known to make their homes in hollow trees, but they can also make burrows in the ground to live or store their food.

Diet

Squirrels are omnivores, such that their diet contains fruits, nuts, berries and even insects.

Fun Facts

  • When it has been snowing, squirrels can find food under a foot/30 centimetres of snow.
  • Squirrels can run up to 20 miles per hour.
  • To escape from predators, squirrels will run in an zigzag pattern to distract and run away from predators making it harder to pounce on them.

Other Types of Squirrels

There are many different types of squirrels, including tree squirrels, ground squirrels, and flying squirrels. Each type has unique adaptations to their environment.
